## Screen Inventory
-   **Splash Screen**: Retro/pixel-art intro.
-   **League Creation**: Admin screen to name and create a league.
-   **Easy Invite Join**: Landing page for invitees to pick a nickname and join.
-   **Dashboard (Matches)**: List of World Cup matches (grouped by date/group).
-   **Prediction Modal/Screen**: Score input for a specific match.
-   **Leaderboard**: Live ranking of users in a league.
-   **Match History/Snapshot**: Personal history of predictions and shareable snapshot.

## Critical UX Decisions
-   **Anonymous First**: Users can join and predict with just a nickname.
-   **WhatsApp Integration**: "Copy/Share to WhatsApp" is a primary action.
-   **Retro/Pixel Art**: Aesthetic must be preserved in production implementation.
-   **No Friction**: Minimal clicks to enter a score.

## States
-   **Match Not Started**: Prediction open.
-   **Match Closing (T-X min)**: Prediction about to close.
-   **Match In Progress**: Prediction closed, live updates.
-   **Match Finished**: Results locked, points calculated.

## UX Notes
-   "viejitos" friendly: large buttons, clear text, no complex navigation.
-   Retro vibe: pixel fonts or bold colors.
