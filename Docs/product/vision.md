# Product Vision: Fuut (FIFA World Cup 2026 Prediction Platform)

## Purpose
Fuut is a web application (PWA) designed to bring friends together through friendly competition during the 2026 FIFA World Cup. It provides a simple, "retro-cool" interface for predicting match results and competing in private leagues.

## Target Audience
-   Football fans looking for a simple way to manage "pollas" (prediction games) with friends and family.
-   Users who want a frictionless experience without mandatory sign-ups (initially).
-   Mobile-first users (WhatsApp/Telegram sharing).

## Success Metric
-   Number of leagues created.
-   Number of active participants per league.
-   High retention of users throughout the tournament.

## Minimum Viable Scope (MVP)
1.  **League Management**: Create leagues, share invite links/codes.
2.  **Easy Invite**: Join a league with just a nickname (anonymous session).
3.  **Predictions**: Predict scores of World Cup matches (up to X mins before kickoff).
4.  **Points System**: Exact score (3 pts), correct winner (2 pts), etc.
5.  **Leaderboards**: Live rankings within each league.
6.  **Match Data**: Full 2026 schedule and results (manual or API-based).

## Constraints & Dependencies
-   Launch must be well before June 11, 2026 (first match).
-   Must be a PWA for low friction.
-   Needs a backend for persisting predictions and leaderboards.
